Top 5 Anime Every Teen Should Watch

Anime picks from a 13-year-old fan. No boring stuff here.

1. My Hero Academia

A superhero school where even the underdogs become legends. Perfect for teens who love action, growth, and powerful moments.

2. Demon Slayer

Beautiful animation, strong emotion, and a fight for family. A top-tier pick with intense battles and heart.

3. Haikyuu!!

Even if you don’t like volleyball, you’ll love the characters and energy. Motivating and super fun.

4. Spy x Family

A spy, an assassin, and a psychic kid pretending to be a family. Action + comedy + heart = greatness.

5. Slam Dunk

Old-school basketball anime that still hits hard. Great for fans of sports and underdog stories.
